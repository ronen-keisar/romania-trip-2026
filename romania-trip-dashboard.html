Day by Day Itinerary
Fri 31 Jul — Arrival & Dinner
Land 17:25, pick up rental car, check in Vila Cotroceni. Easy dinner near the apartment, no big activity.
Option A
Pergola Cotroceni · 4.7-4.9★ · vegetarian/vegan options confirmed · 0.6mi walk
Option B
Olea · 4.9★ (314+) · vegan/gluten-free confirmed · ~15min drive, near JW Marriott
Sat 1 Aug — Shopping
AFI Cotroceni Mall — literally next to the apartment and the Botanical Garden, easy walk, wide range of shops and a food court.
Sun 2 Aug — Old Town Bucharest
Lipscani · Stavropoleos Church · Caru' cu Bere for lunch · Cișmigiu Gardens in the afternoon (paddle boats, playgrounds)
Mon 3 Aug — Salt Mine en route
Drive to Poiana Mărului via Salina Slănic Prahova (~1h45 from Bucharest). Karting, ping-pong, billiards, playground inside, constant 12-13°C.
Tue 4 Aug — Local Nature Day
Hiking trail or lake near Poiana Mărului, relaxed pace.
Wed 5 Aug — Bran & Răsnov
Bran Castle · Răsnov Fortress
Thu 6 Aug — Waterpark
Aquatic Paradise Brasov — indoor/outdoor pools, slides, saunas, well-rated for families, good rainy-day backup too.
Fri 7 Aug — Brasov Old Town
Tâmpa cable car · Old town · Brasov Adventure Park (rope park) if energy allows
Sat 8 Aug — Two Families, Older Kids
Just the two families this day, Agam (8.5) is the youngest here, so this works well for a slightly bigger activity.
Option A: Bear watching
Bear hide tour from Brasov — no hiking, sit-and-wait in a secure hide, near-guaranteed sightings (most groups see 5+), evening start (16:00-18:00). Some operators set a minimum age of 12, others have taken kids as young as 7, worth confirming with the specific operator before booking.
Option B: Rafting
Easygoing, yes for the right operator. The closest base (Buzau river near Nehoiu, ~1.5hrs from Brasov) accepts children from age 7, described as level 2-3 (moderate, beginner-friendly with calmer recovery stretches between rapids), guided with full safety gear and training included. Good fit for your group.
Sun 9 Aug — Drive to Sinaia, All 3 Families
Back to the full group today. Drive Poiana Mărului → Sinaia (~1.5 hrs) with a stop at Castelul Cantacuzino in Busteni, en route. Neo-Romanian style castle (1911), known as the real-life filming location for Nevermore Academy in Netflix's "Wednesday." Highlights: guided interior tour (~30 min, mostly in Romanian with QR-code audio for other languages), a terrace with sweeping Bucegi Mountains views, gardens with fountains and modern art, a small kids' adventure park, and a zipline. Easy stop, not strenuous, good photo spot for the kids if they know the show.
Also possible today
Pełeş Castle could also be done this day instead of Aug 10, if you want to split the Sinaia castle visit and pool time across both days rather than cramming Peleş into one. Either day works, just needs a booked timed-entry slot at bilete.peles.ro.
Mon 10 Aug — Peleș Castle
Pełeş Castle (book online in advance, August is peak season, sells out 1-3 weeks ahead, timed entry slots, capped at 2000/day). Afternoon at hotel pool/spa.
Tue 11 Aug — Drive to Bucharest, Detour
Drive Sinaia → Bucharest. Good detour: Castelul Cantacuzino (if not done Aug 9) or Snagov Monastery (Vlad the Impaler's legendary burial site, scenic lake, roughly on the way). Check into Downtown Jungle Vibes.
Wed 12 Aug — Therme București
Therme București — full water park/wellness day, indoor/outdoor pools, saunas, relaxation zones.
Thu 13 Aug — Palace of Parliament + Shopping
Palace of Parliament tour — advance booking recommended, can be done online or by phone, time slots fill up. Afterward, shopping slot: Unirii Shopping Center (central, walkable from Parliament) or back to AFI Cotroceni if more convenient.
Fri 14 Aug — Open Day
Saturday is the chill/wrap-up day, so use Friday for something concrete: Herastrau Park + boat ride + Village Museum, or a final dinner at one of the well-rated vegan-friendly old town spots like Aubergine or Simbio.
Sat 15 Aug — Open / Departure
Late checkout (until 11:00), relaxed morning, pack, drop off rental, flight 20:45.
