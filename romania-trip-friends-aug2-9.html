Old Town Bucharest SUN AUG 2
Lipscani · Stavropoleos Church · Caru' cu Bere for lunch · Cișmigiu Gardens in the afternoon (paddle boats, playgrounds)

Salt Mine en Route + Drive to Poiana Mărului MON AUG 3
Drive to Poiana Mărului via Salina Slănic Prahova (~1h45 from Bucharest, roughly on the way north). Karting, ping-pong, billiards, and a playground inside, plus a constant 12–13°C microclimate. Continue to Casa Raul, arriving evening.
☀️ ~30°C Bucharest start · ~23°C Poiana Mărului on arrival
★ Casa Raul base ★
Local Nature Day TUE AUG 4
Main Activity · Zărnești Cliffs
Zărnești Cliffs (Prăpăstiile Zărneștiului) — a dramatic limestone gorge with towering narrow walls and a mostly flat, shaded gravel trail (~1 hr each way), family-friendly. Gateway to Piatra Craiului National Park.
Optional Add-on · Cheese Farm
Traditional sheepfold (stână) in the Bran–Moeciu hills — watch shepherds make and smoke telemea/caș cheese, taste fresh dairy, and meet the flock. Rustic and very kid-friendly; call ahead to arrange a visit.
⛅ ~23°C / 12°C · Poiana Mărului, seasonal avg
Bran Castle & Râșnov Fortress WED AUG 5
Bran Castle · Răsnov Fortress
⛅ ~24°C / 13°C · Brasov area, seasonal avg
Waterpark THU AUG 6
Aquatic Paradise Brasov — indoor/outdoor pools, slides, saunas, well-rated for families, good rainy-day backup too.
⛅ ~24°C / 13°C · Brasov, seasonal avg (indoor option if rain)
Horseback Riding FRI AUG 7
Main Activity · Horseback Riding
Poiana Brasov Horse Riding Center is well-reviewed for families, has ponies for younger riders and full horses for older kids/adults, forest trails, and a playground on site for downtime between rides.
Optional Evening Add-on · Bear Watching
Bear hide tour from Brasov — no hiking, sit-and-wait in a secure hide, near-guaranteed sightings (most groups see 5+), evening start (16:00–18:00), pairs well after an active daytime ride. Confirm minimum age with operator, policies range from 7+ to 12+.
🚚 Family 1 moves to Brasov today — checking out of Vibe House Madisson (Poiana Mărului) and relocating to Brasov for the rest of the stay.
⛅ ~23°C / 12°C · Brasov area, seasonal avg · evenings cool, bring a layer
Brasov Old Town SAT AUG 8
Old town · hike up to the Brasov sign on Mt. Tâmpa (about an hour, moderate, reviewers consistently rate it better than the cable car, which has long queues and a dated, cramped ride) · Brasov Adventure Park (rope park) if energy allows
⛅ ~24°C / 13°C · Brasov, seasonal avg
Families 1+2+3 SUN AUG 9
Back to the full group today. Drive Poiana Mărului → Sinaia (~1.5 hrs) with a stop at Castelul Cantacuzino in Bușteni, en route. Neo-Romanian castle (1911), known as the real-life filming location for Nevermore Academy in Netflix's "Wednesday." Guided interior tour (~30 min), a terrace with Bucegi Mountains views, gardens with fountains and modern art, a small kids' adventure park, and a zipline.
Castle Option
Pełeş Castle could be done this day, splitting the Sinaia castle visit and pool time across the stay. Needs a booked timed-entry slot in advance.
Non-Castle Option · Waterfall Hike
Cascada Urlătoarea — an easy, well-marked forest trail from the Bșteni cable car area, about an hour round trip, family-friendly with kids and older walkers alike, free, no tickets or queues.
⛅ ~24°C / 13°C · Sinaia/Busteni, seasonal avg
