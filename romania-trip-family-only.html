Day by Day Itinerary
Fri 31 Jul — Arrival & Dinner
Land 17:25, pick up rental car, check in Vila Cotroceni. Easy dinner near the apartment: Pergola Cotroceni (4.7-4.9★, vegan/vegetarian options confirmed, 0.6mi walk).
Sat 1 Aug — Shopping
AFI Cotroceni Mall — next to the apartment and the Botanical Garden, easy walk, wide range of shops and a food court. Optional: Cotroceni on Ice, the mall's indoor ice rink, open year-round in 90-minute sessions, kids would love it.
Sun 2 Aug — Old Town Bucharest (2 Families)
Lipscani · Stavropoleos Church · Caru' cu Bere for lunch · Cișmigiu Gardens in the afternoon · Museum of Senses Bucharest (near the Botanical Garden, interactive/sensory exhibits, good fit for kids)
★ Aug 3 – 9 (3 Families) ★
Mon 3 Aug — Salt Mine en Route
Drive to Poiana Mărului via Salina Slănic Prahova (~1h45 from Bucharest). Karting, ping-pong, billiards, playground inside, constant 12–13°C. Continue to Casa Raul, arriving evening.
★ Casa Raul base ★
Tue 4 Aug — Local Nature Day
Hiking trail or lake near Poiana Mărului, relaxed pace.
Wed 5 Aug — Bran & Răsnov
Bran Castle · Răsnov Fortress
Thu 6 Aug — Waterpark
Aquatic Paradise Brasov — indoor/outdoor pools, slides, saunas, good rainy-day backup too.
Fri 7 Aug — Brasov Old Town
Old town · hike up to the Brasov sign on Mt. Tâmpa (about an hour, moderate, better rated by reviewers than the cable car) · Brasov Adventure Park (rope park) if energy allows
Sat 8 Aug — Families 2+3
Main Activity · Horseback Riding
Poiana Brasov Horse Riding Center — well-reviewed for families, ponies for younger riders, full horses for older kids/adults, forest trails, playground on site for downtime.
Optional Evening Add-on · Bear Watching
Bear hide tour from Brasov — no hiking, sit-and-wait in a secure hide, near-guaranteed sightings (most groups see 5+), evening start (16:00–18:00). Confirm minimum age with operator, policies range from 7+ to 12+.
Sun 9 Aug — Families 1+2+3, Drive to Sinaia
Back to the full group today. Drive Poiana Mărului → Sinaia (~1.5 hrs) with a stop at Castelul Cantacuzino in Bușteni, en route. Neo-Romanian castle (1911), the real-life filming location for Nevermore Academy in Netflix's "Wednesday." Guided interior tour (~30 min), terrace with Bucegi Mountains views, gardens with fountains, a kids' adventure park, zipline.
Castle Option
Pełeş Castle — needs a booked timed-entry slot in advance, August sells out 1-3 weeks ahead.
Non-Castle Option · Waterfall Hike
Cascada Urlătoarea — easy, well-marked forest trail near Bșteni, about an hour round trip, family-friendly, free, no tickets or queues.
Mon 10 Aug — Sinaia
Whichever of Pełeş Castle / waterfall hike wasn't done Aug 9 can fill this day instead. Afternoon at hotel pool/spa.
Tue 11 Aug — Drive to Bucharest, Detour
Drive Sinaia → Bucharest. Good detour: Castelul Cantacuzino (if not done Aug 9) or Snagov Monastery (Vlad the Impaler's legendary burial site, scenic lake). Check into Downtown Jungle Vibes.
Wed 12 Aug — Therme București
Therme București — full water park/wellness day, indoor/outdoor pools, saunas, relaxation zones.
Thu 13 Aug — Palace of Parliament + Shopping
Palace of Parliament tour — advance booking recommended. Afterward, shopping at Fashion House Outlet Centre Militari — ~60 stores including Nike, Adidas, Puma, and Guess, daily discounts of 30-90%, plus a kids' play area. A different spot from the Aug 1 mall, so no repeat.
Fri 14 Aug — Open Day
Saturday is the chill/wrap-up day, so use Friday for something concrete: Herastrau Park + boat ride + Village Museum, or a final dinner at Aubergine or Simbio.
Sat 15 Aug — Open / Departure
Late checkout (until 11:00), relaxed morning, pack, drop off rental, flight 20:45.
